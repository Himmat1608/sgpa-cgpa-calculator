How SGPA is Calculated

SGPA = Σ (Credit × Grade Point) / Σ Credits

Example Calculation

Subject A: 4 credits × Grade A+ (10) = 40

Subject B: 3 credits × Grade A (9) = 27

Subject C: 6 credits × Grade C (5) = 30

Total Credits = 13

Total Points = 97

SGPA = 97 / 13 = 7.46
